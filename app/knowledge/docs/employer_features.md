# ZyncJobs Employer Features

## Job Posting
- Post job openings with full details: title, description, skills, salary, location, job type
- AI-assisted Job Description (JD) Generator: generate professional JDs from a job title
- Bulk job posting: upload multiple jobs at once
- Set application deadlines and job expiry
- Refresh job postings to keep them visible

## Candidate Search
- Search candidates by skills, experience level, location, and education
- AI-powered candidate matching: automatically find candidates that fit your job
- Filter by availability, notice period, and job type preference
- View candidate profiles, resumes, and ATS scores

## AI Candidate Ranking
- ZyncJobs automatically ranks candidates for each job posting
- Ranking is based on: skill match, experience, education, ATS score, location, and recency
- Explainable ranking: see why each candidate was ranked at their position
- No manual shortlisting needed — AI does the heavy lifting

## Interview Management
- Schedule interviews directly from the platform
- Send interview invitations to candidates
- Track interview status: scheduled, completed, cancelled
- Integrate with Google Meet for virtual interviews

## Team Collaboration
- Add team members to your employer account
- Assign jobs to specific team members
- Role-based access: Owner, Manager, Recruiter
- Shared candidate pipeline across the team

## Analytics Dashboard
- View total applications, interviews scheduled, hired, and rejected
- Track job performance: views, applications per job
- Monitor recruitment pipeline metrics

## How to Get Started as an Employer
1. Sign up at ZyncJobs with your company email
2. Choose "Employer" as your role
3. Set up your company profile
4. Post your first job
5. Review AI-ranked candidates and start hiring
