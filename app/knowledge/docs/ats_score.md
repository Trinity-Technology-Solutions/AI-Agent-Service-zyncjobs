# ATS Score on ZyncJobs

## What is ATS Score?
ATS Score on ZyncJobs measures how well your resume will perform when processed by automated screening software used by employers. It evaluates formatting, keywords, skills match, and grammar quality.

## How It Works
ZyncJobs analyzes your resume against industry best practices. The score ranges from 0 to 100. A score above 80 means your resume is highly ATS-compatible.

## Factors That Affect Your Score
- Standard section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
- Keyword density matching your target role
- File format (PDF preferred)
- No tables, columns, images, or charts
- Consistent date formatting
- Proper spelling and grammar
- Quantified achievements
- Complete contact information

## How to Check
1. Upload your resume to ZyncJobs.
2. Enter a job description (optional but recommended).
3. Click Check ATS Score.
4. Review your score and missing keywords.
5. Apply suggestions to improve.

## Tips
- Use keywords from the job description naturally.
- Avoid headers and footers for important info.
- Use standard fonts like Arial or Calibri.
- Save as PDF, not DOCX or image.
