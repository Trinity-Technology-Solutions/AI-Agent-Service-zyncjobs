# Recruiter Guide on ZyncJobs

## How ZyncJobs Helps Recruiters
ZyncJobs provides AI-powered tools for employers and recruiters to post jobs, find candidates, and manage hiring efficiently.

## Posting a Job
1. Register as an employer on ZyncJobs.
2. Go to Post a Job from your employer dashboard.
3. Enter job title, description, requirements, and skills.
4. Set location, job type, experience level, and salary range.
5. Publish the job to make it visible to candidates.

## Finding Candidates
- Use AI-powered candidate search to filter by skills, experience, location.
- View candidate profiles with resume summaries and ATS scores.
- Rank candidates based on match percentage with your job requirements.
- Shortlist and message candidates directly.

## Bulk Job Import
Employers can upload multiple jobs at once using CSV or XML files through the Bulk Job Import feature. The system parses and validates each job before publishing.

## Recruiter Actions
- Review applications and track their status.
- Schedule interviews with candidates.
- Send bulk messages to shortlisted candidates.
- View analytics on job performance and candidate response rates.

## AI Recruiter Assistant
The AI Recruiter helps generate job descriptions, suggests candidate matches, and provides hiring insights.
