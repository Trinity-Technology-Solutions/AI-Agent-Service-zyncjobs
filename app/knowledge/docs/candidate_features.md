# ZyncJobs Candidate Features

## Job Search
- Search jobs by title, skill, location, job type, and experience level
- AI-powered job recommendations based on your profile and skills
- Filter by full-time, part-time, remote, hybrid, contract, internship
- Save jobs and set job alerts for new openings
- View job details including salary, skills required, and company info

## Resume Tools
- Upload your existing resume (PDF or DOCX)
- AI Resume Parser: automatically extracts your skills, experience, and education
- ATS Score Checker: checks how well your resume matches job descriptions
- Resume Improvement: AI suggests specific improvements to increase your ATS score
- Resume Builder: build a professional resume from scratch using AI guidance

## ATS Score
- ATS (Applicant Tracking System) score shows how well your resume will pass automated screening
- Score is based on: formatting, keywords, skills match, experience, education, and grammar
- ZyncJobs gives you a score out of 100 with specific improvement tips
- Higher ATS score = better chance of getting shortlisted

## Career Roadmap
- Get a personalized career roadmap based on your current skills and target role
- AI identifies the gap between where you are and where you want to be
- Roadmap includes: skills to learn, certifications to get, projects to build, timeline

## Skill Gap Analysis
- Compare your current skills against a target job role
- AI identifies missing skills and recommends how to learn them
- Suggests online courses, certifications, and practical projects

## Interview Preparation
- AI generates interview questions based on your target role and resume
- Practice behavioral questions using the STAR method
- Get technical questions specific to your tech stack
- Receive feedback and model answers

## Application Tracking
- Track all your job applications in one place
- See application status: applied, shortlisted, interview scheduled, hired, rejected
- Get notifications for status updates

## How to Get Started as a Candidate
1. Sign up at ZyncJobs with your email
2. Choose "Job Seeker" as your role
3. Complete your profile: add skills, experience, education, and job preferences
4. Upload your resume for AI analysis
5. Start searching and applying for jobs
