# ATS Score & Resume Analysis on ZyncJobs

## What is ATS?
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software used by employers to automatically screen resumes before a human reads them. If your resume doesn't pass the ATS, it never reaches the recruiter.

## How ZyncJobs ATS Score Works
ZyncJobs analyzes your resume across 6 dimensions:

| Category       | Weight | What it checks |
|----------------|--------|----------------|
| Skills Match   | 25%    | Do your skills match the job requirements? |
| Keywords       | 20%    | Are relevant industry keywords present? |
| Experience     | 20%    | Is your experience clearly described with achievements? |
| Formatting     | 15%    | Is the resume clean, readable, and ATS-friendly? |
| Education      | 10%    | Is your education section complete? |
| Grammar        | 10%    | Is the language professional and error-free? |

**Total: 100 points**

## Score Ranges
- **90-100:** Excellent — very likely to pass ATS screening
- **75-89:** Good — minor improvements recommended
- **60-74:** Average — needs improvement in keywords and formatting
- **Below 60:** Poor — significant changes needed

## How to Improve Your ATS Score
1. **Add a Skills section** — list all relevant technical and soft skills
2. **Use keywords from the job description** — match the exact phrasing
3. **Quantify achievements** — use numbers: "increased sales by 30%", "managed team of 8"
4. **Use standard section headings** — Experience, Education, Skills, Projects
5. **Avoid tables and columns** — ATS systems can't read complex layouts
6. **Use action verbs** — led, developed, implemented, optimized, designed
7. **Keep formatting simple** — no graphics, no text boxes, no headers/footers with important info
