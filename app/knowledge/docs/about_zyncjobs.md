# About ZyncJobs

ZyncJobs is an AI-powered recruitment platform that connects job seekers (candidates) with employers (recruiters) using artificial intelligence and automation.

## What is ZyncJobs?
ZyncJobs is a smart hiring platform built to make recruitment faster, smarter, and more efficient. It uses AI to match candidates with the right jobs, analyze resumes, generate career roadmaps, and help employers find the best talent.

## Who built ZyncJobs?
ZyncJobs was built by a team of engineers and AI specialists focused on transforming the recruitment industry using modern AI technology.

## Who is ZyncJobs for?
- **Job Seekers (Candidates):** People looking for jobs, wanting to improve their resumes, prepare for interviews, or plan their career growth.
- **Employers (Recruiters):** Companies and hiring managers who want to post jobs, find candidates, and manage their hiring pipeline efficiently.

## Is ZyncJobs free?
ZyncJobs is free for candidates to use. Employers can sign up and post jobs with available plans.

## What makes ZyncJobs different?
- AI-powered job matching (not just keyword search)
- Resume ATS score checker with improvement suggestions
- AI career roadmap and skill gap analysis
- Interview preparation with AI-generated questions
- Smart candidate ranking for employers
- All-in-one platform: jobs + resume + career + hiring
