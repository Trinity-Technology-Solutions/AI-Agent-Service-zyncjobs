# Interview Preparation on ZyncJobs

## How ZyncJobs Helps with Interviews
ZyncJobs offers interview preparation tools including mock interviews, common question banks, and AI-powered feedback to help candidates ace their interviews.

## Types of Interview Questions Covered
- Behavioral questions (Tell me about yourself, Why this company?)
- Technical questions based on your skills (coding, system design, domain knowledge)
- HR questions (salary expectations, availability, strengths and weaknesses)
- Role-specific questions for Java, Python, React, Node.js, Data Science, DevOps, and more

## How to Prepare
1. Go to Interview Tips from your candidate dashboard.
2. Select your target role and experience level.
3. Browse curated questions with sample answers.
4. Practice with mock interview mode.
5. Review AI feedback on your answers.

## Behavioral Questions Framework (STAR Method)
- Situation: Describe the context.
- Task: What was your responsibility?
- Action: What steps did you take?
- Result: What was the outcome? Use numbers where possible.

## Technical Interview Tips
- Review core concepts of the technologies listed on your resume.
- Practice coding problems on platforms like LeetCode and HackerRank.
- Be ready to explain your past projects in detail.
- Prepare questions to ask the interviewer about the team and tech stack.
