# How to Apply for Jobs on ZyncJobs

## Applying for a Job
1. Search for jobs using keywords, location, or category.
2. Click on a job listing to view details.
3. Click Apply Now on the job page.
4. Upload your resume or select one from your saved resumes.
5. Add a cover letter (optional but recommended).
6. Submit your application.

## After Applying
- Track your application status in your candidate dashboard.
- Statuses: Applied, Under Review, Shortlisted, Interview Scheduled, Rejected, Offered.
- You can withdraw an application if needed.
- Employers may message you through ZyncJobs.

## Quick Apply
Some jobs offer Quick Apply, which uses your default resume and profile to apply with one click.

## Tips for Successful Applications
- Tailor your resume for each job.
- Write a specific cover letter mentioning the company name.
- Apply within 48 hours of a job posting for best visibility.
- Complete your profile 100% to increase match chances.
- Set up job alerts to receive new matching jobs by email.

## Job Alerts
1. Go to Job Alerts in your settings.
2. Set keywords, location, and frequency.
3. Receive email notifications when matching jobs are posted.
