# Career Tools on ZyncJobs

## Career Roadmap
The Career Roadmap feature creates a personalized step-by-step plan to help you reach your target job role.

### How it works:
1. Tell ZyncJobs your current role and target role (e.g., "I'm a Junior Developer, I want to become a Senior Full Stack Engineer")
2. AI analyzes your current skills vs. what the target role requires
3. ZyncJobs generates a roadmap with:
   - Skills to learn (in priority order)
   - Certifications to get
   - Projects to build for your portfolio
   - Estimated timeline (e.g., 6-12 months)
   - Learning resources

### Example Roadmap (Junior → Senior Full Stack):
- Month 1-2: Master React advanced patterns, TypeScript
- Month 3-4: Learn Node.js, REST APIs, PostgreSQL
- Month 5-6: Learn Docker, CI/CD basics, AWS fundamentals
- Month 7-8: Build 2 full-stack portfolio projects
- Month 9-10: Contribute to open source, get AWS certification
- Month 11-12: Apply for Senior roles, prepare for system design interviews

## Skill Gap Analysis
Skill Gap Analysis identifies exactly what skills you're missing for a target role.

### How it works:
1. Your current skills are extracted from your resume
2. You specify a target job title or paste a job description
3. AI compares your skills against the requirements
4. ZyncJobs shows:
   - Skills you already have (matched)
   - Skills you're missing (gap)
   - Priority order for learning missing skills
   - Recommended courses and resources

### Example:
Target: Data Scientist
Your skills: Python, SQL, Excel
Missing: Machine Learning, TensorFlow, Statistics, Data Visualization (Tableau/PowerBI)
Recommendation: Start with Statistics → then Scikit-learn → then TensorFlow

## Interview Preparation
ZyncJobs AI generates interview questions tailored to your role and experience.

### Types of questions generated:
- **Technical questions:** Based on your tech stack and target role
- **Behavioral questions:** STAR-method questions (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- **System design questions:** For senior engineering roles
- **HR questions:** Common HR and culture-fit questions

### How to use:
1. Tell ZyncJobs the role you're interviewing for
2. Optionally paste the job description
3. AI generates 10-20 relevant questions
4. Practice answering and get model answers for reference

### STAR Method:
- **S**ituation: Describe the context
- **T**ask: What was your responsibility?
- **A**ction: What did you do?
- **R**esult: What was the outcome?

## Salary Insights
ZyncJobs provides salary data based on real job listings in its database.

- Average salary for a role
- Salary range (min to max)
- Salary by experience level (Entry, Mid, Senior, Lead)
- Market benchmarks

Use salary insights to negotiate your offer or understand market rates before applying.
