# Interview Preparation on ZyncJobs

## How ZyncJobs Helps You Prepare
ZyncJobs offers tools and resources to help you prepare for interviews including AI mock interviews, common questions, and tips.

## Mock Interviews
- Practice with AI-powered mock interviews.
- Choose from technical, behavioral, or HR rounds.
- Receive feedback on your answers, tone, and clarity.
- Unlimited attempts to improve your performance.

## Common Interview Questions
- Tell me about yourself.
- Why do you want this role?
- What are your strengths and weaknesses?
- Describe a challenge you overcame.
- Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
- Why should we hire you?

## Tips for Success
- Research the company before the interview.
- Practice your answers out loud.
- Use the STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result.
- Prepare questions to ask the interviewer.
- Dress professionally for video interviews.
- Test your camera and microphone beforehand.
- Follow up with a thank-you email within 24 hours.

## Technical Interview Prep
- Review data structures and algorithms.
- Practice coding problems on ZyncJobs.
- Understand system design basics.
- Review your resume projects and be ready to discuss them.

## Behavioral Interview Prep
- Identify 5 key stories from your experience.
- Align stories with common competencies: leadership, teamwork, problem-solving.
- Keep stories concise (60-90 seconds each).
