# ZyncJobs Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

**What is ZyncJobs?**
ZyncJobs is an AI-powered recruitment platform that helps candidates find jobs and employers hire talent using artificial intelligence.

**Is ZyncJobs free for candidates?**
Yes. ZyncJobs is completely free for job seekers. You can search jobs, upload your resume, check your ATS score, and use career tools at no cost.

**How do I create an account?**
Go to ZyncJobs, click Sign Up, enter your email and password, and choose your role (Job Seeker or Employer).

**Can I use ZyncJobs on mobile?**
Yes. ZyncJobs is accessible on mobile browsers.

## Resume & ATS

**What is an ATS score?**
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. An ATS score tells you how well your resume will pass automated screening by employers. ZyncJobs scores your resume out of 100 based on formatting, keywords, skills, experience, and education.

**How do I improve my ATS score?**
Upload your resume on ZyncJobs. The AI will analyze it and give specific suggestions: add missing keywords, fix formatting, add a skills section, quantify achievements, etc.

**What file formats does ZyncJobs accept for resumes?**
ZyncJobs accepts PDF and DOCX resume formats.

**How does the AI Resume Parser work?**
When you upload your resume, ZyncJobs AI extracts your contact info, skills, work experience, education, and projects automatically. This data is used to match you with relevant jobs.

## Job Search

**How does AI job matching work?**
ZyncJobs compares your skills, experience, and preferences against job requirements using AI. It ranks jobs by how well they match your profile — not just by keyword.

**How do I apply for a job on ZyncJobs?**
Search for a job, click on it to view details, and click Apply. Your profile and resume are submitted to the employer.

**Can I save jobs and apply later?**
Yes. Click the Save button on any job listing to save it to your profile.

## Career Tools

**What is the Career Roadmap feature?**
The Career Roadmap feature creates a personalized plan to help you reach your target job role. It shows what skills to learn, certifications to get, and projects to build — with a timeline.

**What is Skill Gap Analysis?**
Skill Gap Analysis compares your current skills with the requirements of a target job. It tells you exactly what skills you're missing and how to learn them.

## Employers

**How do I post a job on ZyncJobs?**
Sign up as an Employer, complete your company profile, and click Post a Job. Fill in the job details or use the AI JD Generator to create a description automatically.

**What is AI Candidate Ranking?**
ZyncJobs automatically ranks all applicants for your job based on skill match, experience, education, ATS score, and other factors. You see the best candidates first.

**Can I add team members to my employer account?**
Yes. Go to Team Settings and invite team members by email. You can assign roles: Owner, Manager, or Recruiter.

## Account & Support

**How do I reset my password?**
Go to the ZyncJobs login page and click Forgot Password. Enter your registered email and follow the instructions.

**How do I contact ZyncJobs support?**
Visit the ZyncJobs Help Center or use the Contact Us page to reach the support team.
