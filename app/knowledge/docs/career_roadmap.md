# Career Roadmap on ZyncJobs

## What is a Career Roadmap?
A Career Roadmap on ZyncJobs is a personalized career progression plan that shows you the skills, experience, and milestones needed to move from your current role to your target role.

## How to Generate a Career Roadmap
1. Log in to your candidate account and go to Career Roadmap.
2. Enter your current role or upload your resume.
3. Enter your target role (e.g., from Junior Developer to Senior Architect).
4. Select your current skills and experience level.
5. Click Generate to get your personalized roadmap.

## What a Roadmap Includes
- Recommended skills to learn with priority order
- Suggested certifications and courses
- Estimated timeline for each milestone
- Experience levels and responsibilities for each stage
- Learning resources (articles, tutorials, practice projects)

## Sample Career Paths
- Junior Developer → Senior Developer → Tech Lead → Engineering Manager
- Data Analyst → Data Scientist → ML Engineer → AI Architect
- QA Tester → Automation Engineer → DevOps Engineer → SRE

## Skill Gap Analysis
ZyncJobs also offers Skill Gap Analysis which compares your current skills against your target role requirements and tells you exactly what to learn next.
